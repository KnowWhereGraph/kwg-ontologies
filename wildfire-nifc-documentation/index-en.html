NIFC Firec back to ToC or Class ToC

IRI: http://stko-kwg.geog.ucsb.edu/lod/ontology/NIFC_Fire

This class denotes point locations for all wildland fires in the United States reported to the IRWIN system. Wildland fire incidents from the IRWIN (Integrated Reporting of Wildland Fire Information) incident service that meet the following criteria: - Categorized as a Wildfire (WF), Prescribed Fire (RX), or Incident Complex (CX) record - Is Valid and not "quarantined" in IRWIN due to potential conflicts with other records
has super-classes
feature c, feature of interest c
has sub-classes
NIFC Incident Complex Fire c, NIFC Prescribed Fire c, NIFC Wildfire c
